Meet the Team

East Winds is led by two founders with deep experience across global strategy, brand, and growth. Together, they bring a rare combination of creative and commercial thinking, shaped inside some of the world’s most influential agencies and organisations.

Meet Lorna Burt
Co-Founder & Strategy Partner

Lorna Burt is a strategic leader with a track record of building and scaling brands through unconventional thinking and rigorous problem solving.

She has held senior roles including Strategy Partner at OPC, Strategy Lead at Bow&Arrow, Strategy Director at Leo Burnett, and Co-Founder at White Crow.

Her work spans global brands and challenger businesses, helping organisations reframe problems, uncover new opportunities, and translate insight into meaningful commercial growth.

At East Winds, she focuses on defining the right questions and cutting through complexity to identify where true growth lies.

Meet Andrew Gibson
Co-Founder & Chief Strategy Officer

Andrew Gibson brings deep global experience in shaping business and brand strategy at the highest level.

Previously Chief Strategy Officer at Creature and Global Strategy Director at McCann, he has worked across international markets, leading strategy for some of the world’s most recognised organisations.

He is known for connecting cultural insight with commercial impact, turning abstract challenges into clear and actionable direction.

At East Winds, he leads the translation of ideas into compelling propositions, ensuring every output is both strategically sound and executable.
